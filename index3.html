About Benj.Resto

Our concept was to build a restaurant that will serve western cuisine, with a western ambience and service standard. Our commitmen to value for money allows the concept to appeal to a wide range of customers without sacrificing food quality and service.


The look and feel of the restaurant is like any traditional western restaurant – there is an unmistakable Western uncomplicated atmosphere that is created utilizing a number of elements including the design, décor, floor and wall finishes, furnishings, table setting, smallware, food presentation, food portions and the menu.


All these combined with warm personal service create a simple and uncomplicated ambience that captures the heart of Filipinos, who love to dine and celebrate with friends and family.
